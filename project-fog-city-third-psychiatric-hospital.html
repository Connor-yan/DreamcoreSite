Notes

“You” woke up in an empty hospital, with all your memories lost. Guided by the NPC “The Sun”, you searched for yourself and learned you had committed suicide here. Fog erodes “The Sun”, splitting it into another persona.

Collage UI + stage-like movement enhance distance when “reading a story”. This is an experiment with meta-narrative.

Fog City Third Psychiatric Hospital — 2.5D Visual Development & Prop Design

Context

Independent 2.5D adventure framed as a “paper-theater” stage. Keeps deliberate distance between player and protagonist to evoke detachment. Year: 2025.

Action

  • Prototyped a side-on 2.5D layout with “paper theater” UI: collage panels, cut-out props, and stage-like blocking to control reveal.
  • Designed narrative beats around amnesia: guidance by the NPC “The Sun,” progressive discovery of self-harm history, and the fog as a corrupting force that splits “The Sun” into another persona.
  • Built a reusable Unity 2.5D toolset: camera rails, scene cards, prop slots, and timing cues for transitions; documented palette, typography, and prop scale rules.

Result

  • Established a consistent visual language (collage UI + stage motion) that sustains the meta-narrative distance.
  • Delivered a visual-dev & prop library enabling fast assembly of new scenes while keeping tone control (haze amount, vignette, light temperature).
