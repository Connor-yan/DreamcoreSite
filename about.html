Standard-Bio · The Hoo

I’m Connor Wu, a game designer/animator exploring slow-burn, atmospheric storytelling without jumpscares. I build surreal, memory-like spaces that blend level design, interaction, and motion to evoke dreamcore and poolcore sensibilities.

00:03
The Philosophy & Skills

My practice treats environment and interaction as narrative: light, sound, and small mechanics become the “voice.” Technically, I work in Unity HDRP (C#, VFX Graph), prototyping with TouchDesigner and MediaPipe for gesture and audio-reactive systems. For 2D, I animate with CSP/AE in a watercolor/ink style, guided by timing and performance. I enjoy turning abstract aesthetics into clear, reproducible pipelines.

00:04
The Proof

Current projects include Dream No More, a meta walking simulator about loops, memory, and depression, featuring a swing interaction, poolcore corridors, and hand-gesture gates. In parallel, DaFeng is a 2D film study inspired by Nuo ritual dance—combining bird symbolism, inheritance, and gentle myth.

00:05
The Future

I’m seeking junior gameplay/technical-artist roles or studio internships where I can prototype mechanics and mood. Let’s collaborate on experiences that feel like memories—quiet, uncanny, and kind.
