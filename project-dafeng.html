大風

DaFeng

Mineral pigments · Fresco wall · Frame-to-frame animation

Overview

After the death of the grandmother who adopted her, a girl inherited the batu dance and, in the ceremony, crossed the boundary between life and death, paying homage to the deities.

Notes

I attempted to express the concept of "regret over life and death" and the reverence for deities through the transformation of symbolic objects and the ritual nature of traditional folk dance: The masks, paper money, bells and steps were constantly translated in the frame, eventually converging into the posture of "watching over".

All the movements are drawn frame by frame, emphasizing the transitions between the symbolic elements, allowing the emotions to gradually emerge in the blank spaces.

DaFeng — 2D Ink-Wash Short (Clip Studio Paint / After Effects)

Context

Personal film study inspired by Nuo ritual dance and the bird motif—examining inheritance, loss, and transformation. Targeted as an animation deliverable within the Folio sequence.

Action

  • Wrote a beat sheet and storyboard to shape the emotional arc (girl → bird → ritual). Drew key and in-between frames in Clip Studio Paint, preserving watercolor/ink textures.
  • Composited in After Effects with depth cues, film grain, and time-remapping to control breath, pauses, and reveal.
  • Iterated via tutor/peer reviews, refining shot blocking, acting poses, and timing; standardized color cues (ink black/ochre/soft blue) and built a small template for cleanup and compositing.

Result

  • Produced cohesive sample sequences that lock the film’s visual language and narrative through-line.
  • Documented a repeatable pipeline (boards → hand-drawn frames → compositing → grade) ready for final cut and scoring.
